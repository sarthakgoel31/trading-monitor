Glossary — Click to expand if you need a refresher
INDICATOR RSI
A "tiredness meter" from 0-100. Above 70 = price went up too much (overbought). Below 30 = price went down too much (oversold). We use 14-period RSI.
SIGNAL RSI Divergence
When price makes a new low but RSI makes a HIGHER low, sellers are losing steam. Price is likely to reverse up. Opposite for bearish.
MEASURE ATR
Average True Range = how much price typically moves per candle. On 6E 5-min, ATR ~ 15 pips. We use it to set stop losses that adapt to volatility.
LEVEL Pivot Level
Key price levels calculated from yesterday's High/Low/Close. Traders worldwide watch these, so price often bounces at them. PP, R1, R2, S1, S2.
LEVEL Fib Retracement
After a big move (say, down), price often bounces back (retraces) to 38.2%, 50%, or 61.8% of that move before continuing. These are high-probability reversal zones.
EXIT Trailing Stop
A stop loss that follows price in your favor but never moves back. If price goes up 30 pips, the stop trails up 30 pips too. Locks in profit automatically.
CONFIRM DXY Correlation
DXY = Dollar strength. 6E = Euro strength. They move OPPOSITE. If DXY is falling, it confirms a 6E long. Think of it like a seesaw.

Your Checklist (need ALL of these)

  • 1. 6E RSI divergence on 5m chart — Price makes new low, RSI makes higher low
  • 2. DXY moving opposite — DXY falling for longs, rising for shorts (the seesaw)
  • 3. Wick rejection in recent candles — Long lower wicks = buyers absorbing sellers
  • 4. Wait for next candle — Must be GREEN for longs, RED for shorts (confirms momentum shifted)
  • 5. Enter at that candle's close — Place trailing stop 1xATR below entry (~15 pips on 6E)

Which candle is which?

Confluence candle (orange box in chart) = the candle where divergence + DXY + wicks all line up. This is where you spot the setup. You do NOT enter here.

Next candle (green box) = the very next 5-min candle. If it closes GREEN (for longs), NOW you enter. If it closes RED — skip, the setup failed.

What if the next candle is a Doji?

A doji (tiny body, long wicks both sides) means indecision — the market hasn't decided yet. Here's what to do:

  • Wait one more candle. If the 3rd candle is GREEN → enter at its close. Your setup is still valid — the doji just delayed confirmation by 5 minutes.
  • If the 3rd candle is also doji or RED → skip. Two candles of indecision after a divergence means the setup is losing momentum. Move on.
  • Never wait beyond the 3rd candle. If it takes more than 2 candles (10 min) to confirm, the divergence is too weak. A good reversal shows its hand quickly.
Summary: Confluence candle → Doji (wait) → Green candle = ENTER
Confluence candle → Doji → Doji/Red = SKIP
Confluence candle → Red = SKIP
Confluence candle → Green = ENTER (ideal)

How the trailing stop works: Your stop follows price up but NEVER moves down. If price goes +30 pips, stop trails to +15 pips. If price reverses, stop locks in profit. No take-profit target needed — the trail does the work.
